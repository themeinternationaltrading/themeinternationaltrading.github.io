Theme International Trading

A place whereby people are empowered and technology advancement is emphasized to produce extraordinary growth in performance and returns.

Theme International Trading SPC, a subsidiary of Theme International Holdings Limited listed on Hong Kong Exchange (0990.HK), is a Singapore-based quantitative trading firm that focuses on improving market efficiency by providing liquidity and price discovery on various derivative markets. We also deploy technology driven trading models to capture arbitrage opportunities and reduce market inefficiencies.

Our team started in 2018 and has now become a leading market-maker in various commodity derivative markets. In 2020, we were honoured and humbled to receive SGX's Most Active 62% Iron Ore Futures Trader 2019 award for being the largest volume market participant.

In pursuance of reaching a wider range of asset classes and products, we constantly seek motivated individuals with strong integrity to join our flat hierarchy organization.
